About CSE

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering which was established in the year 2010 as a part of the Womenâ€™s Engineering College has been empowering the girl students with the required attitudes, aptitudes, skills and abilities necessary for embarking on a fruitful and rewarding career in the field of Computer Science and Information Technology. The initial intake was 60 students which was subsequently increased to 120 students. After serving the cause of women empowerment for a decade, the college evolved into a Co-educational institution. The Department of CSE during the process of teaching, training and transforming the lives of students has itself undergone growth and refinement necessary for educational instruction, delivery and innovation in a student-centric paradigm. The department of CSE which has always played a proactive role in institutionalizing the virtues of technical excellence and professional ethics reiterates its longstanding commitment to the cause of providing value-added education as enshrined in the Organizational Vision and Mission statements.


Computer Science Engineering

The department endeavors to equip the student with the required theoretical foundation and practical skills needed to make them active participants in the highly rewarding field of Computer Science and Information Technology. State-of-the-art infrastructure combined with a team of highly qualified and committed teachers who strive unceasingly to enable students achieve their goals characterize the academic environment. Information and Communication Technology tools that ease the Teaching-Learning process and significantly enhance the quality of subject delivery are used by the teachers on a regular basis.


Students are trained using the pedagogical techniques of participatory, collaborative and experiential learning by way of seminars, workshops, programming clubs including Google Student Developer club and Microsoft Learn Ambassador Coding Club which provide the students with the first-hand experience in learning and refining their skills so as to meet the industry standards.These activities are organized on a regular basis by the Student branch of Computer Society of India so that students gain a first-hand account from the experts in those domains. An awareness is brought about into the minds of the students by way of collaborative research activity with their teachers so that students gain an overview of the manner in which concepts are conceived, designed, prototyped, tested, peer-reviewed and accepted.


The department provides adequate opportunities for students for knowledge exploration from various sources such as Massively Online Open Courseware, NDL lectures from IITs in addition to the regular classroom teaching. Guest-lectures by successful personalities in the field of Computer Science constantly motivate the students and reinforce the fact that efforts expended in the right direction always result in success.


Most importantly, the department strives to provide training in domain skills necessary for refining the skill of the students in order to match industry expectations. The department also facilitates the conduct of training classes to the students in domain skills and soft skills in association with the Skill Development Centre of the college which immensely helps the students in fine tuning their skills in performing well in various placement drives conducted in the college campus or elsewhere.


The department takes pride in empowering the student community and constantly reinforces its commitment to ensuring that each student who gains admission realizes their true potential and gains the needed technical and life skills needed to a successful life and career after graduation. The CSE Department heartily welcomes students to join us and experience the Joy of Learning.

Vision And Mission

Department Vision

To become an educational ecosystem that acts as a beacon for those inclined towards solving societal problems in an inclusive, ethical and technologically relevant manner and steer them towards national development.


Deaprtment Mission

To provide the students with the overall knowledge base essential to enable them to choose domains in which they would like to be trained sufficiently in order to achieve their personal and professional goals. To bring about an awareness in the minds of the students regarding the right approaches to be followed to acquire the necessary skills in the chosen domain. To inculcate the philosophy of Continuous and Never Ending refinement and so that these skills are refined so as to match industry requirements. To evolve a system of content creation, delivery and testing so as to match global educational standards. To create an environment conducive for collaborative research, participatory and student-centric learning, and innovation.

Program Educational Objectives

PEO 1. Acquire, Apply and exhibit the skills required to design, develop and implement solutions for real life problems.


PEO 2. Excel in professional career, higher education and research.


PEO 3. Demonstrate professionalism, entrepreneurship, ethical behavior, communication skills and collaborative team work to adapt the emerging trends by engaging in lifelong learning.



Program Objectives

PO 1.Engineering knowledge: Apply the knowledge of mathematics, science, engineering fundamentals, and an engineering specialization to the solution of complex engineering problems.


PO 2.Problem analysis: Identify, formulate, research literature, and analyze complex engineering problems reaching substantiated conclusions using first principles of mathematics, natural sciences, and engineering sciences.


PO 3.Design/development of solutions: Design solutions for complex engineering problems and design system components or processes that meet the specified needs with appropriate consideration for the public health and safety, and the cultural, societal, and environmental considerations.


PO 4.Conduct investigations of complex problems Use research-based knowledge and research methods including design of experiments, analysis and interpretation of data, and synthesis of the information to provide valid conclusions.


PO 5.The engineer and society Apply reasoning informed by the contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal and cultural issues and the consequent responsibilities relevant to the professional engineering practice


PO 6.Modern tool usage: Create, select, and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering and IT tools including prediction and modeling to complex engineering activities with an understanding of the limitations.


PO 7.Environment and sustainability Understand the impact of the professional engineering solutions in societal and environmental contexts, and demonstrate the knowledge of, and need for sustainable development.
