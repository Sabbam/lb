About Us

12+ Years Experience

Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering encourages and promotes education and keeps its main focus on Student empowerment and their personality development with which they can face the challenges of modern world. The broad objective of the institution is to provide the right opportunities to the students for learning the higher skills in new technologies and building their extraordinary careers in industry and research organizations.

Acclaimed by the student community as an outstanding engineering college in the smart city Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering (for women), was established by The Society for Collegiate Education in 2010 with the objective to produce women technologists and scientists who would engage in research, design and development to help building the nation. Our Campus is ideally located in the heart of the city on 10 acre land.

Detailed Description


Profile

Profile

As an extended wing of Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya Colleges, Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya College of Engineering was established to provide multi disciplinary education facility in the same campus by The Society for Collegiate Education in July 2010 with the prior approval of AICTE, Government of Andhra Pradesh & Andhra University. The college endeavors to meet the strong demand in industries and research organizations for Competent Engineers, Proficient Technologists and Creative Scientists. The Campus is ideally located in the heart of the city on 10 acre land and it boasts of providing various services, facilities and amenities for the all round progress of inquisitive learners.



The Broad Objective of the institution is to provide the right opportunities to engineering students for learning the higher skills in new technologies and building their extraordinary careers in industry and research organizations. The general program objectives are to prepare students for entrance into professional careers. Proper care is taken to develop student’s overall personality, bringing out the students hidden virtues and making them technically competent to face the knowledge challenges of 21st century.
