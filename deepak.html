Dr. Lankapalli Bullayya College has a long proud history of glorious academic service and it has been a leader and innovator in professional education. A chain of Junior College, Degree College, P.G. College and Engineering College in our campus provide higher education at all levels. It has played an important role in the all-round development of the youth and in grooming competent leaders.

Dr, L. Bullayya College of Engineering seeks to provide students with skills and strengths through intellectual and technical competence. The proficient professors ignite their imagination. Staff and administration assist them in their knowledge pursuits. Our campus ambience and its beautiful surroundings stimulate their minds, broaden their intellectual horizons and inspire their hearts.

The Academic Programs offered by different departments of our college are of good standards. The faculty and staff of our college feel honored to partner with the prospective students in their educational journey. The governing body, management, faculty, staff, students and alumnae of Dr. L Bullayya College of Engineering, encourage you to learn more about us by visiting our campus.
