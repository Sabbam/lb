Dr. D. Madhavi

Professor

Biography

Dr.D.Madhavi working as Professor in Dr. LankapalliBullayya College of Engineering for women, Visakhapatnam. She has 22 years of teaching experience atvarious colleges for both undergrad and grad Computer Science and Engineering students. She obtained her doctoral degree in Computer Science Engineering from Acharya Nagarjuna University, Andhra Pradesh and Postgraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering from University College of Engineering (A), Andhra University, Andhra Pradesh. Her research interests include Automated Reasoning, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Analytics.
